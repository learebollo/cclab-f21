Welcome to the Image-ine Gallery

I hope that this project will allow you to experience a variety of different emotions based on the way you choose to interact with its pixel generation

The rules are simple:

1. Begin the experience

2. Watch the image generation

3. Play music if you wish

4. Play around with the range sliders which control the pixel size, r, g and b values respectively.

5. Feel something and Enjoy!
